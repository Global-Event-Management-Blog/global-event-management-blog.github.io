## Eid Al Adha 2026 Dates and Significance

In 2026, Eid Al Adha is expected to begin on the evening of **Friday, May 29**, and continue through to **Saturday, May 30**, subject to official moon sightings. This festival—commonly known as the Festival of Sacrifice—marks a period of deep cultural significance, bringing families, communities, and organizations together in reflection and celebration.

Scheduling business conferences in the Middle East or luxury group events around Eid requires careful consideration. Many companies and government offices observe public holidays, creating unique opportunities and challenges for event logistics, venue sourcing, and corporate hospitality.

Conversely, scheduling major conferences or gala dinners during Eid may impact attendance due to family commitments.
