## The Dubai Temperature Advantage in Event Planning

Dubai enjoys a subtropical desert climate, offering over 300 days of sunshine each year. While the summer temperatures can soar, the cooler months from October to April present ideal conditions for outdoor and indoor gatherings. This seasonal predictability is a key asset for professional event planners looking to deliver flawless business conferences, gala dinners, and executive retreats.
